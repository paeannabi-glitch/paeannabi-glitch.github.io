About

I am an anthropologist, writer, photographer, and communications professional currently pursuing an MA in Medical Anthropology and Mental Health at SOAS University of London. Before moving to London, I completed my BSS in Anthropology at the University of Dhaka.

My journey with writing began while I was still in school. Over the years, I have written for Prothom Alo, The Daily Star, Earki, and other platforms. Professionally, I have worked with organisations including BRAC, the Centre for Policy Dialogue (CPD), and the South Asian Network on Economic Modeling (SANEM), alongside experience in advertising and communications.
